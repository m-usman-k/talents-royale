Talents Royale Participation Agreement

This Participation Agreement governs all individuals and entities engaging with Talents Royale, including performers, contractors, clients, and partners. By participating in any capacity, you agree to the following terms:

1. Eligibility Requirements

Participation in Talents Royale is open to individuals of all ages and from any country worldwide. By entering, participants affirm that:

  • They have the legal capacity to participate in accordance with the laws of their country of residence.
  • If under the age of 18, they have obtained verifiable consent from a parent or legal guardian.
  • They agree to abide by all platform rules, including video submission guidelines, voting protocols, and community standards.
  • They understand that all disputes will be governed by the laws of the State of Georgia, USA, and resolved in Henry County.
  • They are responsible for any tax obligations, customs duties, or legal restrictions in their home country related to participation or prize receipt.

2. Performance Expectations

Participants must follow tier-based performance guidelines, including video duration limits, content standards, and submission protocols. All acts must reflect the values of Talents Royale and comply with safety, decency, and platform rules.

3. Code of Conduct

Talents Royale is committed to fostering a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for all participants, judges, and audience members. By participating, you agree to uphold the following standards:

  • Respectful Behavior: Treat all individuals—online and offline—with dignity and professionalism. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discriminatory remarks based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, or identity will not be tolerated.

This agreement is legally binding. By participating in Talents Royale, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these terms and conditions.
